2nd SEPTEMBER 2023

8am - 10am

Our Great Ocean Road day trip begins in Melbourne, the capital of Victoria.The first leg of our journey took us along the Surf Coast, known for its famous surf beaches and relaxed coastal towns. With the sun now fully above the horizon, we cruised along the Surf Coast. At 8:00 AM, we reached Jan Juc, where the salty breeze and the sound of crashing waves invigorated us. A little later, at 9:00 AM, we found ourselves at the world-famous Bells Beach lookout, capturing the breathtaking view.

10:30am - 11:30am

By 10:30 AM, we arrived in Aireys Inlet and embarked on the ascent of the Split Point Lighthouse. The clock showed 11:15 AM when we reached the top, greeted by panoramic views that were nothing short of spectacular. The timeless beauty of this place left us in awe.

12:00pm - 1:30pm

We ventured further along the road, arriving in Lorne just in time for lunch. At 12:45 PM, we were savoring a delicious meal at a local café, soaking in the coastal views. Time ticked on, but we were in no hurry – this was a day to savor.

2:30pm - 4:30pm

The most anticipated part of our journey awaited in the afternoon. At 2:30 PM, we stood before the Twelve Apostles, towering limestone stacks rising from the sea. We spent the next two hours exploring, capturing photos from Gibson Steps, Loch Ard Gorge, and the Razorback. The golden sun illuminated these natural wonders, etching the images into our memory.

5pm - 6pm

As the clock approached 5:00 PM, we continued our drive, discovering the Arch and London Arch. Each formation was a testament to the timeless forces of nature. The Arch, with its natural bridge, was particularly captivating. We couldn't help but think of the millennia it took to shape these wonders.

6:30 PM - 8:30 PM

With the day waning, we reluctantly began the journey back to Melbourne at 6:30 PM. The drive was a poetic encore to the day's adventures, offering a different perspective of the coastline. As the clock neared 8:30 PM, we returned to the city, our hearts full of gratitude for this unforgettable day.
